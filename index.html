Introduction:
Hi. My name is Roger Kirkness. My email is: me@rogerkirkness.com.

Work:
I work at Shopify on creating new markets. My current focus is wholesale ecommerce.

Education History:
I was unschooled until I was twelve by my parents. I went to middle school at twelve and stayed until I completed high school at sixteen. I decided at sixteen I wanted to start and run companies so I hustled an internship at GNC to learn about business.

Work History:
I started at GNC as an unpaid intern helping with marketing. I got promoted a few times and ended up starting and briefly running the Canadian ecommerce division. I left GNC to join the family business, Minotaur Software. I spent three years learning about all aspects of the company, and I primarily did sales day to day.

After that time I stopped taking a salary and started a software company called Catchmate that made food processing plant floor internet of things software. It went okay and I learned a great deal. In the end I merged it back into Minotaur when I had some customers.

Around the time that I merged Catchmate back into Minotaur I got an offer to join Shopify and do what I had done at GNC and Minotaur: create new markets. I am currently working at Shopify developing new markets. I am also researching ideas in my other areas of interest.

Interests
My main interests are people systems, technological progress, macroeconomics, neuroscience, psychoneuroimmunology and business. I read almost entirely nonfiction books, mostly about one of the things above and the people who contribute to them.
